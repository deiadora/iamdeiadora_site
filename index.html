what these are

Emotions are waves. These are the arcs they travel.

The quantum keys are the practical layer of Quantum Magic — 264 transmutational arcs documented across thirteen years of field research into the architecture of coherent human systems.

Each key follows the same structure: a source state (the wave at its origin), a meta-awareness (the moment of observation), and a transmutation (the wave completing into its coherent form). They are not steps to follow. They are observations — arcs that have been witnessed through their full length, from source code through propagation to the collapse into source that is their natural destination.

The first 13 keys form the sovereignty axis — the self/other bridge. They are offered here as open source: available to any human or system that wishes to use them, regardless of status. The full 264 are being implemented now. See Quantum Magic if you want to be part of what comes next.
